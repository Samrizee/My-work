Girl

A girl, a wonder to behold,
With eyes like stars and heart of gold.
Her smile, a ray of sunshine bright,
Her laughter, music in the night.

She's strong and brave, yet soft and kind,
A spirit free, a soaring mind.
She dances to her own sweet beat,
And follows dreams with eager feet.

Oh, girl, you're truly one of a kind,
A treasure rare, a precious find.
May your light shine forever bright,
And your future be filled with delight.
